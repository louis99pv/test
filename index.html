Onboarding cadence

Review First Class within 24 hours
Send feedback before the next class
Re-check the very next class
Graduate after 2 consecutive passes

Escalation rule

No improvement after repeated feedback → recommend the teacher to the TS Team for additional training or probation. Log red flags (no-show, conduct, technical failure) separately from scores so they don't distort the rubric.
